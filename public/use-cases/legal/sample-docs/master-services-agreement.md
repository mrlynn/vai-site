# Master Services Agreement

**Effective Date:** January 15, 2025
**Agreement Number:** MSA-2025-0042

This Master Services Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into by and between Acme Corp, a Delaware corporation with its principal place of business at 1200 Innovation Drive, Suite 400, San Francisco, CA 94107 ("Provider"), and the entity identified on the applicable Order Form ("Customer"). Provider and Customer are each referred to herein as a "Party" and collectively as the "Parties."

## 1. Definitions

1.1 "Affiliate" means any entity that directly or indirectly controls, is controlled by, or is under common control with a Party, where "control" means ownership of more than fifty percent (50%) of the voting securities of such entity.

1.2 "Confidential Information" means all non-public information disclosed by one Party to the other Party in connection with this Agreement, whether disclosed orally, in writing, or by any other means, that is designated as confidential or that a reasonable person would understand to be confidential given the nature of the information and the circumstances of disclosure.

1.3 "Deliverables" means any work product, reports, documentation, or other materials created by Provider for Customer in connection with the Services as specified in an Order Form or Statement of Work.

1.4 "Intellectual Property Rights" means all patents, copyrights, trademarks, trade secrets, and any other intellectual property rights recognized in any jurisdiction worldwide, including applications and registrations for any of the foregoing.

1.5 "Order Form" means any ordering document executed by the Parties that references this Agreement and specifies the Services to be provided, applicable fees, and other relevant terms.

1.6 "Services" means the professional services, consulting, implementation, or other services to be performed by Provider as described in an Order Form or Statement of Work.

1.7 "Statement of Work" or "SOW" means a document executed by both Parties that describes the specific Services, Deliverables, timelines, milestones, and associated fees for a particular engagement.

## 2. Scope of Services

2.1 **General.** Provider shall perform the Services described in each Order Form and SOW executed by the Parties. Each Order Form and SOW shall be governed by the terms of this Agreement and, together with this Agreement, shall constitute the entire agreement between the Parties with respect to the subject matter of that Order Form or SOW.

2.2 **Standard of Performance.** Provider shall perform all Services in a professional and workmanlike manner, consistent with generally accepted industry standards and practices. Provider shall assign qualified personnel with the skills and experience necessary to perform the Services.

2.3 **Change Orders.** Either Party may request changes to the scope of Services described in an SOW. Any such changes shall be documented in a written change order signed by authorized representatives of both Parties. No changes to the scope of Services shall be effective unless agreed upon in writing.

2.4 **Customer Cooperation.** Customer shall provide Provider with reasonable access to Customer's personnel, systems, facilities, and information as reasonably necessary for Provider to perform the Services. Customer acknowledges that Provider's ability to perform the Services may depend on Customer's timely cooperation.

2.5 **Subcontractors.** Provider may engage subcontractors to perform portions of the Services, provided that Provider shall remain responsible for the performance of such subcontractors and their compliance with the terms of this Agreement.

## 3. Fees and Payment

3.1 **Fees.** Customer shall pay Provider the fees specified in each Order Form or SOW ("Fees"). Unless otherwise specified in the applicable Order Form, all Fees are quoted in United States Dollars.

3.2 **Invoicing.** Provider shall invoice Customer in accordance with the billing schedule set forth in the applicable Order Form or SOW. If no billing schedule is specified, Provider shall invoice Customer monthly in arrears for Services performed during the preceding calendar month.

3.3 **Payment Terms.** Customer shall pay all undisputed invoices within thirty (30) days of the date of invoice. Payments shall be made by wire transfer or ACH to the account designated by Provider.

3.4 **Late Payments.** Any undisputed amounts not paid when due shall accrue interest at a rate of one and one-half percent (1.5%) per month, or the maximum rate permitted by applicable law, whichever is less, from the date such payment was due until the date paid.

3.5 **Taxes.** All Fees are exclusive of applicable taxes. Customer shall be responsible for all sales, use, value-added, withholding, and similar taxes arising from the transactions contemplated by this Agreement, excluding taxes based on Provider's net income.

3.6 **Expense Reimbursement.** Customer shall reimburse Provider for all reasonable, pre-approved, out-of-pocket expenses incurred in connection with the performance of Services, provided that Provider submits appropriate documentation of such expenses.

## 4. Intellectual Property

4.1 **Provider IP.** Provider retains all right, title, and interest in and to (a) all Intellectual Property Rights owned by Provider prior to the Effective Date, (b) all tools, methodologies, frameworks, and know-how developed by Provider independently of this Agreement, and (c) any general knowledge, skills, or experience acquired by Provider during the performance of Services (collectively, "Provider IP").

4.2 **Customer IP.** Customer retains all right, title, and interest in and to all Intellectual Property Rights owned by Customer prior to the Effective Date and all data, content, and materials provided by Customer to Provider in connection with the Services ("Customer IP").

4.3 **Deliverables.** Subject to Section 4.1 and Customer's payment of all applicable Fees, Provider hereby assigns to Customer all right, title, and interest in and to the Deliverables, excluding any Provider IP incorporated therein. To the extent any Provider IP is incorporated into the Deliverables, Provider grants Customer a non-exclusive, worldwide, perpetual, irrevocable, royalty-free license to use such Provider IP solely as part of the Deliverables.

4.4 **Feedback.** If Customer provides any suggestions, ideas, enhancement requests, or other feedback regarding Provider's products or services ("Feedback"), Provider shall have a royalty-free, worldwide, irrevocable, perpetual license to use and incorporate such Feedback into Provider's products and services.

## 5. Representations and Warranties

5.1 **Mutual Representations.** Each Party represents and warrants that: (a) it has the legal power and authority to enter into this Agreement; (b) this Agreement constitutes a valid and binding obligation of such Party; and (c) the execution and performance of this Agreement will not conflict with any other agreement to which such Party is a party.

5.2 **Provider Warranties.** Provider represents and warrants that: (a) the Services will be performed in a professional and workmanlike manner consistent with generally accepted industry standards; (b) the Deliverables will conform in all material respects to the specifications set forth in the applicable SOW; and (c) to Provider's knowledge, the Deliverables will not infringe any third party's Intellectual Property Rights.

5.3 **Disclaimer.** EXCEPT AS EXPRESSLY SET FORTH IN THIS AGREEMENT, PROVIDER MAKES NO WARRANTIES OF ANY KIND, WHETHER EXPRESS, IMPLIED, STATUTORY, OR OTHERWISE, AND PROVIDER SPECIFICALLY DISCLAIMS ALL IMPLIED WARRANTIES, INCLUDING ANY IMPLIED WARRANTIES OF MERCHANTABILITY, FITNESS FOR A PARTICULAR PURPOSE, OR NON-INFRINGEMENT, TO THE MAXIMUM EXTENT PERMITTED BY APPLICABLE LAW.

## 6. Term and Termination

6.1 **Term.** This Agreement shall commence on the Effective Date and shall continue in effect until terminated by either Party in accordance with this Section 6 ("Term").

6.2 **Termination for Convenience.** Either Party may terminate this Agreement upon sixty (60) days' prior written notice to the other Party. Termination of this Agreement shall not affect any Order Form or SOW that is then in effect, which shall continue in accordance with its terms until completed or terminated.

6.3 **Termination for Cause.** Either Party may terminate this Agreement or any Order Form or SOW immediately upon written notice if the other Party: (a) materially breaches this Agreement and fails to cure such breach within thirty (30) days after receiving written notice of the breach; or (b) becomes insolvent, files for bankruptcy, or has a receiver appointed for a substantial portion of its assets.

6.4 **Effect of Termination.** Upon termination or expiration of this Agreement: (a) Customer shall pay Provider for all Services performed and expenses incurred through the effective date of termination; (b) each Party shall return or destroy all Confidential Information of the other Party; and (c) Sections 1, 3 (with respect to accrued obligations), 4, 5.3, 6.4, and any other provisions that by their nature should survive, shall survive termination.

6.5 **Transition Assistance.** Upon Customer's request made within thirty (30) days after termination, Provider shall provide reasonable transition assistance to Customer for a period not to exceed ninety (90) days, at Provider's then-current rates for such services.
