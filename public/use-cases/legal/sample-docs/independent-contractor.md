# Independent Contractor Agreement

**Acme Corp**
**Effective Date:** January 15, 2025

This Independent Contractor Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into by and between Acme Corp, a Delaware corporation with its principal place of business at 400 Technology Drive, Suite 200, San Francisco, California 94105 ("Company"), and the contractor identified on the signature page hereto ("Contractor").

## 1. Engagement

1.1 The Company hereby engages Contractor, and Contractor hereby accepts engagement, to perform the services described in the attached Statement of Work ("SOW"), which is incorporated herein by reference.

1.2 Contractor shall commence work on the date specified in the SOW and shall complete all deliverables within the timeframes set forth therein unless the parties agree in writing to an extension.

1.3 The Company may issue additional Statements of Work from time to time, each of which shall be governed by the terms of this Agreement upon execution by both parties.

1.4 Contractor shall perform all services in a professional and workmanlike manner, consistent with industry standards and in compliance with all applicable laws, regulations, and ordinances.

## 2. Deliverables and Milestones

2.1 Contractor shall deliver the work products, reports, and materials specified in the SOW ("Deliverables") according to the milestone schedule set forth therein.

2.2 Each Deliverable shall be subject to the Company's review and acceptance. The Company shall have fifteen (15) business days following receipt of a Deliverable to review and either accept the Deliverable or provide written notice of deficiencies. If the Company provides notice of deficiencies, Contractor shall correct such deficiencies within ten (10) business days at no additional cost.

2.3 A Deliverable shall be deemed accepted upon written confirmation by the Company or, in the absence of written notice of deficiencies, upon expiration of the fifteen (15) business day review period.

2.4 Contractor shall provide regular progress updates to the Company's designated project manager at intervals specified in the SOW, or weekly if no interval is specified.

## 3. Compensation

3.1 The Company shall compensate Contractor for services performed under this Agreement at the rates specified in the applicable SOW, payable within thirty (30) days of the Company's receipt of a properly submitted invoice.

3.2 Invoices shall include a detailed description of the services performed, the hours worked (if applicable), the applicable rate, and any pre-approved expenses. Invoices shall be submitted to the Company's accounts payable department in the format specified by the Company.

3.3 Contractor shall be solely responsible for all taxes, including income taxes, self-employment taxes, and any other taxes arising from compensation received under this Agreement. The Company shall not withhold any taxes from payments to Contractor.

3.4 The Company shall reimburse Contractor for reasonable, pre-approved, out-of-pocket expenses incurred in connection with the performance of services, provided that such expenses are documented with receipts and submitted in accordance with Company policy.

## 4. Intellectual Property Assignment

4.1 All work product, inventions, discoveries, improvements, designs, documentation, software, code, and other materials created, developed, or conceived by Contractor in the course of performing services under this Agreement ("Work Product") shall be the sole and exclusive property of the Company.

4.2 Contractor hereby irrevocably assigns to the Company all right, title, and interest in and to the Work Product, including all intellectual property rights therein, including patents, copyrights, trademarks, trade secrets, and any other proprietary rights, in all jurisdictions worldwide.

4.3 To the extent that any Work Product includes any pre-existing intellectual property of Contractor ("Contractor IP"), Contractor hereby grants to the Company a perpetual, irrevocable, worldwide, royalty-free, fully paid-up, non-exclusive license to use, reproduce, modify, distribute, and create derivative works of such Contractor IP as incorporated in the Work Product.

4.4 Contractor shall execute all documents and take all actions reasonably requested by the Company to perfect, register, and enforce the Company's rights in the Work Product.

## 5. Indemnification

5.1 Contractor shall defend, indemnify, and hold harmless the Company and its officers, directors, employees, agents, successors, and assigns (collectively, "Indemnified Parties") from and against any and all claims, damages, losses, liabilities, costs, and expenses (including reasonable attorneys' fees) arising out of or related to: (a) Contractor's breach of any representation, warranty, or obligation under this Agreement; (b) Contractor's negligence or willful misconduct in the performance of services; (c) any claim that the Work Product infringes or misappropriates any third party's intellectual property rights; or (d) any failure by Contractor to comply with applicable laws or regulations.

5.2 The Company shall provide Contractor with prompt written notice of any claim for which indemnification is sought. Contractor shall have the right to control the defense and settlement of any such claim, provided that Contractor shall not settle any claim in a manner that imposes any obligation on the Company without the Company's prior written consent.

5.3 The Company shall cooperate with Contractor in the defense of any claim and shall make available to Contractor all relevant records and personnel at Contractor's reasonable request and expense.

## 6. Insurance

6.1 Contractor shall maintain, at Contractor's own expense, the following insurance coverage throughout the term of this Agreement: (a) commercial general liability insurance with minimum limits of one million dollars ($1,000,000) per occurrence; (b) professional liability (errors and omissions) insurance with minimum limits of one million dollars ($1,000,000) per claim; and (c) workers' compensation insurance as required by applicable law.

6.2 Upon request, Contractor shall provide the Company with certificates of insurance evidencing the required coverage and naming the Company as an additional insured on the commercial general liability policy.

## 7. Relationship of the Parties

7.1 Contractor is an independent contractor and not an employee, agent, partner, or joint venturer of the Company. Nothing in this Agreement shall be construed to create an employment relationship, partnership, agency, or joint venture between the parties.

7.2 Contractor shall have no authority to bind the Company or to make any representations or commitments on behalf of the Company. Contractor shall not hold themselves out as an employee or agent of the Company.

7.3 Contractor retains the right to determine the manner, method, and means by which services are performed, subject to the Company's right to specify the desired results and deliverables.

7.4 Contractor is free to perform services for other clients during the term of this Agreement, provided that such engagements do not conflict with Contractor's obligations to the Company or involve the use or disclosure of the Company's Confidential Information.

## 8. Term and Termination

8.1 This Agreement shall commence on the Effective Date and shall continue until the completion of all services described in the applicable SOW, unless earlier terminated as provided herein.

8.2 Either party may terminate this Agreement upon thirty (30) days' written notice to the other party. The Company may terminate this Agreement immediately upon written notice if Contractor materially breaches any provision of this Agreement and fails to cure such breach within ten (10) days of receiving written notice.

8.3 Upon termination, Contractor shall promptly deliver to the Company all completed and in-progress Work Product, and the Company shall pay Contractor for all services satisfactorily performed through the effective date of termination.

**IN WITNESS WHEREOF**, the parties have executed this Agreement as of the date first written above.
